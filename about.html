Who We Are

A Healthier Smile Is a Happier Life

At The Family Dental Care, we believe that a healthy smile is a reflection of a happy life. Our clinic was founded with a singular vision — to make premium dental care accessible, comfortable, and genuinely caring for every member of your family.

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of Gurgaon's trusted dental destinations, we have always put our patients first. Our approach blends clinical excellence with genuine warmth — because we know that visiting the dentist can feel daunting, and we're here to change that experience entirely.

Every smile we treat is unique, and every patient deserves personalised attention. That's the promise we make — and keep — every single day.

Our Team

Meet Your Dentists

Passionate, experienced, and dedicated to giving you the very best dental care possible.

Dr. Rishikesh Mishra

Principal Dentist & Founder

BDS (PGIDS Rohtak), Fellowship in maxillofacial and dental trauma

Dr. Rishikesh Mishra is a dedicated and skilled dental surgeon committed to delivering high-quality, patient-centered care. A graduate of PGIDS, Rohtak, he combines clinical expertise with a gentle and ethical approach to dentistry.

With additional training through a Fellowship in Maxillofacial and Dental Trauma, Dr. Mishra is well-equipped to handle dental emergencies and complex cases with confidence and precision. His areas of focus include restorative dentistry, aesthetic treatments, and preventive oral care, ensuring that patients not only receive treatment but also understand how to maintain long-term oral health.

Dr. Mishra strongly believes in patient education and emphasizes the importance of early diagnosis, regular dental check-ups, and personalized treatment planning. His goal is to create a comfortable environment where patients feel informed, confident, and cared for. At The Family Dental Care, he strives to provide modern, reliable, and compassionate dental services for patients of all ages.

BDS Implantology 5+ Yrs Experience

Dr. Khushboo Pandey

Dental Surgeon & Cosmetic Dentistry Specialist

BDS

Dr. Khushboo Pandey is a compassionate dental surgeon dedicated to promoting preventive and prophylactic oral care. She believes that maintaining oral health begins with awareness, early diagnosis, and consistent care.

With a strong focus on prevention, Dr. Pandey emphasizes the importance of regular dental check-ups, professional cleanings, and patient education. She takes a personalized approach to guide patients on proper oral hygiene practices, helping them prevent dental issues before they develop.

Her gentle and patient-friendly nature ensures a comfortable experience for individuals of all ages. She is committed not only to treating dental concerns but also to empowering patients with the knowledge needed for long-term oral health.
